About Me

Hi! I am Luise but my friends call me Lul. I’m from Denmark, born in Helsingør, moved to Copenhagen, met David in Rome and settled down in Stockholm in 2008. I have a Bachelor degree in Social studies from University College of Copenhagen from 2008. We started Green Kitchen Stories in 2009. In 2014 I graduated as a Dipl. Holistic Nutritional Therapist at School of Nutritional Medicine in Stockholm. I’m the lucky mom of 3 wild & vegetable eating kids. I love everything green from broccoli to smoothies.
